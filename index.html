Fractal Fantasy: Liquid Entropy Information Design: - Abstract information design - No visible navigation or text-based guidance - The interactive elements emerge as users begin to move their cursor, which is hinted by the shifting in light, motion and 3D depth - Visual hierarchy is defined through scale and motion - Proximity and white space - A canvas dedicated to the sensory experience - Control panel on the top right corner

Mapping: - Control panel on the top right corner, consists of sliders to control the data - Consists of a checkbox and sliders, alongside a reset button - You are able to tell that it’s a slider because the horizontal layout of the slider with a contrasting colour for the slider button itself - However, the main way to “activate” the sound is intuitive and implicit. There are no separate buttons, sliders or labels. Rather the user moves the mouse around the page to explore what sections make what sounds. Moving the cursor around alters visual depth and spatial audio
